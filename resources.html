Resources

Developers maintaining this spec and its reference implementations are often found on RocketChat. You might also connect with them via the Hyperledger Aries mailing list, in Hyperledger working groups, in DIF and W3C meetings, or at the semi-annual Internet Identity Workshop or Rebooting Web of Trust conferences. They sometimes answer questions tagged with decentralized-identifiers, decentralized-identity, hyperledger-aries, or hyperledger-indy on stackoverflow.com.
